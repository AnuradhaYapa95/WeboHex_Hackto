Hi, fancy contributing to the Grouparoo Project?





Requirements:
2 merged/accepted PRs: Flyte Mug
3 merged/accepted PRs or a video: Flyte T-Shirt
Full-fledged integration or a blog: Voucher or “The North Face®” Flyte Jacket
If your contribution adds value to Flyte heavily, they will customize your swag and add you as a Flyte contributor!

How to sign up:
Fill out this form after you are done with either of the challenges,
on or before October 31st, 2021. Join Flyte’s Slack #hacktoberfest2021 channel
if you have any questions.

Notes:
Check out their blog for more info. The first 15 contributors will get a mug irrespective
of the of merged pull requests.

Issues:
Hacktoberfest-tagged Flyte issues
